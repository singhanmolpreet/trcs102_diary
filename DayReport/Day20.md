## Key Learnings

* Deep Learning uses neural networks with multiple layers to learn complex patterns.
* A Perceptron is the fundamental building block of neural networks.
* Activation functions introduce non-linearity, enabling neural networks to solve complex problems.
* Different activation functions are suitable for different types of learning tasks.
